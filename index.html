What is HackFresno?

HackFresno is a student hackathon at Fresno State that encourages creativity and innovation while solving some of the world's toughest problems. Stay up for 24 hours with the coolest and brightest people you will ever meet, and help design a product that can improve the world.

Everyone is welcome, from the most experienced developers and designers to the first-time enthusiasts who may have never opened a terminal window, but have a drive to solve our biggest challenges. Regardless of your skill, there is a place for you at HackFresno.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a nonstop 24-hour design and innovation competition. Students can develop projects that solve real-world problems in teams over the course of the event. At the end of the hackathon, the completed projects are displayed to judges and other participants.

So, we're not breaking into anyone's computers right?

What does it cost to attend?

Nothing at all! We provide all meals, snacks, and swag for free, so that all you have to worry about is what you'll be building.

So what do I bring with me?

You will need your student ID, government ID (to check out hardware),as well as your own computer and any other small hardware you may want to hack with. The event is overnight, so you may want to also bring a sleeping bag and pillow, as well as some toiletries. Please do not bring any cots, tents or large camping gear!

What if I'm a beginner?

Not to worry! We will have beginning hacking workshops at the start of the event. There will also be tech talks and mentors present throughout to help you with your ideas.

How do teams work?

Teams can be up to four members. If you don't have a team, don't worry! We will help you find a team once you get to the event.
